1. Arriving at the School
You and Eldrin arrive at the castle gates. The grand school looms ahead, its spires casting long shadows in the twilight. Despite the damage, the school retains its breathtaking majesty, with faint magical auras shimmering along its edges.

Eldrin pats your shoulder, his gaze thoughtful but firm. “Go on, young one. Head to the auditorium and join the others. I must ensure the professors and the rest of the grounds are secure.” With a reassuring nod, he turns and strides toward the central tower, his tattered cloak trailing behind him.
